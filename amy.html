Tips for surviving Term 3

1. Revise with a buddy!

The best way to stay moivated during revision period is to grab your seminar bestie and help each other out. Maybe you could test each other on learning facts or quotes. Head to Leamington's best coffee shops to spice up your revision session - this way it feels more like a lunchdate. With a coffee and cake, revision never felt so good.

2. Sleep should be your new best friend

During the exam period it may seem tempting to pull that notorious 'all-nighter', but getting plenty of sleep is essential for brain power. Staying up all night to cram may hinder your productivity the next day. Aim for at least 7 hours sleep when revising, I promise you'll feel better the next day.

3. Eat well and keep hydrated

I'd recommend keeping a 1 or 2 litre bottle with you so you can keep hydrated in the library. This will fight fatique and keep you feeling refreshed so you can have all the brain power you need!
